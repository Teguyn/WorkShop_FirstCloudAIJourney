### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
A dynamic, modern, and highly tech-driven working environment. The open workspace helped me quickly integrate and provided the best space to focus on researching and developing projects.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The Mentors and Admin team are extremely enthusiastic, dedicated, and highly qualified. Mentors are always ready to guide the system architecture, troubleshoot deep technical bugs, and provide highly constructive feedback, while the Admin team coordinates activities and connects members very thoughtfully. Furthermore, the knowledge shared is highly practical and beneficial for long-term career development.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
Perfect alignment with my Software Engineering major. The program provided hands-on opportunities with real-world problems, helping apply core knowledge to concrete solutions on the Cloud platform. In particular, accessing advanced AI services like Bedrock and integrating GenAI into products helped broaden my horizons and update me with the latest tech trends.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
This is a major launching pad to comprehensively elevate my capability. I gained access and opportunities to learn the AWS ecosystem (Serverless, Container), prompt engineering on Amazon Bedrock, while training my DevOps mindset and project management skills according to enterprise standards.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The corporate culture emphasizes proactiveness, a progressive mindset, and professional conduct. Open to opinion sharing and open-minded thinking. Team spirit is clearly demonstrated through discussions, aligning viewpoints, and smooth coordination between modules (Frontend, Backend, AI).

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company offers flexible hours, enabling students to balance study and work effectively.


### Other Feedback
- Sincere thanks to AWS Vietnam and the program organizers for their dedication in building such a meaningful First Cloud AI Journey. The program has not only changed my technical mindset but also helped us grow significantly in our professional working style.
- Huge thanks to the mentors who accompanied and enthusiastically supported me throughout the internship. The knowledge, experience, and guidance from you all are invaluable assets that make me more confident on my path of personal and career development.
